Before we begin, who do you think it is when you hear this music?
That’s right. You are correct, we will be introducing J.K. Rowling. The author of the famous Harry Potter book series.
Her actual name is Joanne Rowling, in short, Jo.
Born in 1965, she grew up in England and South-East Wales. She lived with her parents and a younger sister.
Jo’s father, Peter, works as an aircraft engineer in Rolls Royce factory in Bristol.
Her mother, Anne, worked as a science technician in the Chemistry department at Wyedean Comprehensive (the school where Jo attended).
Jo grew up surrounded by books and she loves reading them!
This is why she aspired to be a writer from an early age. She started her first step in writing her first book, Rabbit, at the age of six.
